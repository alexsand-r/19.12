About Autoplatfroma: A Deep Dive into Our Company's Background

Our company offers unique and modern services in the field of vehicle selection, purchase, and worldwide delivery right to your doorstep. Every day, our experts and AI algorithms tirelessly search for advantageous vehicles, tailored to the individual preferences and budgets of hundreds of clients at US and Canadian auto auctions.

Our logistics specialists secure the best delivery rates, ensuring your vehicle reaches your home country without any hidden fees. We provide you with exclusive opportunities, guaranteeing comfort, reliability, and transparency in the global car-buying market.

Our services are available to customers from 62 countries

We have extensive experience in worldwide shipping, allowing us to offer affordable car delivery services tailored to your needs, ensuring your vehicle arrives safely and on time.

Before we offer any car to our customers, it goes through a detailed inspection by a group of experts who are skilled in different areas of the car industry. At the end of this process, our AI system checks the car based on many factors and predicts if buying it will be a smart choice.

We do this to make sure every car in our selection is carefully checked by both professionals and advanced technology.

Change the process of buying a car

Car auctions in the USA and Canada are becoming increasingly popular every day. This is due to the rising prices of cars in European countries, making purchasing a vehicle from abroad more cost-effective. We provide you with a free opportunity to explore available cars directly on our website, choose your favorite, and place a bid at the car auction after a free consultation with our expert.

With this option, you not only save money on the car's actual cost but also gain full control over the process, allowing you to choose a vehicle that perfectly fits your needs. Additionally, by relying on a single company for all services, you avoid unnecessary commissions, further contributing to the overall affordability of the car.
